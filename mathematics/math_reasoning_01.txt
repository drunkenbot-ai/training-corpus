A shop has ten pens.
Four pens are sold.
Six pens are left.
Ten minus four equals six.

Multiplication is repeated addition.
Three groups of four make twelve.
Four plus four plus four equals twelve.
Three times four equals twelve.

Division shares things equally.
Twelve biscuits are shared by three children.
Each child gets four biscuits.
Twelve divided by three equals four.

A fraction is part of a whole.
Half means one of two equal parts.
If a pizza has two equal slices, one slice is one half.
One half plus one half makes one whole.

A decimal can show a part.
Zero point five means one half.
Zero point two five means one quarter.
One point zero means one whole.

A percentage is a part of one hundred.
Fifty percent means fifty out of one hundred.
Fifty percent is one half.
Twenty five percent is one quarter.

A ratio compares amounts.
There are two red balls and three blue balls.
The ratio of red to blue is two to three.

An average shares a total equally.
Sara reads two books.
Mina reads four books.
Together they read six books.
The average is three books.

A rectangle has length and width.
Area tells how much space is inside.
A rectangle is five meters long.
It is three meters wide.
The area is fifteen square meters.

A triangle has three sides.
A square has four equal sides.
A circle has no corners.

Time can be measured.
Sixty seconds make one minute.
Sixty minutes make one hour.
Twenty four hours make one day.

Money can be added and subtracted.
Ravi has ten dollars.
He spends three dollars.
He has seven dollars left.

Probability tells how likely something is.
A coin has two sides.
One side is heads.
One side is tails.
If the coin is fair, heads and tails are equally likely.

An equation has two equal sides.
x plus two equals five.
x must be three.
Three plus two equals five.

A train moves sixty kilometers each hour.
It travels for four hours.
Sixty times four equals two hundred forty.
The train travels two hundred forty kilometers.

Maya buys six notebooks.
Each notebook costs two dollars.
Six times two equals twelve.
Maya pays twelve dollars.

A farmer has twenty eggs.
He packs them in boxes of five.
Twenty divided by five equals four.
He needs four boxes.

A class has eighteen students.
Half are girls.
Half of eighteen is nine.
There are nine girls.

Check work by reversing it.
Eight plus seven equals fifteen.
Fifteen minus seven equals eight.
